Types of Information Collected

We collect two categories of information: personal data and non-personal data.

  • Personal Data: When you register on our site, place an order, subscribe to our newsletter, participate in surveys or contests, or complete a form, we collect personal data from you. This data may consist of your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and payment details.
  • Non-Personal Data: We gather non-personal data, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the pages you visit on our site. This information helps us enhance our website's performance and user experience.

Utilization of Your Information

We utilize your private data for an array of reasons, including:

  • Processing and completing your orders
  • Providing order updates, promotional content, and customized offers
  • Enhancing our website, products, and services based on your input
  • Addressing customer service inquiries and support requirements
  • Managing site features such as contests, promotions, and surveys
  • Confirming your identity and preventing fraudulent activities

We may also use your non-personal data to examine trends, collect demographic information, and optimize the overall functionality of our website.

Cookies and Additional Tracking Technologies

To improve your experience on our website, we employ cookies and other tracking technologies. You can opt to disable cookies through your browser settings; however, doing so may restrict some features of our website.
